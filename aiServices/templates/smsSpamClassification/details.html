Necessity?

Now a days everyone has a cell phone in their pocket with basic features such as messaging and calling. Spam communications are those that are delivered to lot of people without their permission and usually promote products, facilities, or business possibilities. The proportion of fraudulent communications among spam has drastically grown in recent times. Fraud messages sometimes use a tempting or misleading offers to fool recipients into providing money or sensitive information.

One approach towards creating a system for scam sms recognition and early scam detection is identifying spam messages. AI enabled Natural Language Processing tools have seized control of the issue and found a solution by enabling us to distinguish between ham and spam text based on past data.
